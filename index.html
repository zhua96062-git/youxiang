About BohaoGroup

BohaoGroup 是一家专注于欧美航线的综合国际物流服务商。 我们深耕整柜订舱（FCL）、专业清关及门到门物流领域， 凭借丰富的行业经验与一手资源优势，为全球贸易商提供高效、可靠、透明的物流解决方案。

BohaoGroup is a comprehensive international logistics provider specializing in Europe & North America routes. With deep expertise in FCL booking, professional customs clearance, and door-to-door delivery, we deliver efficient, reliable, and transparent logistics solutions for global traders.
